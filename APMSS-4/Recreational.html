Opening & Closing Ceremony

Faculty and staff from the Medical School are invited to deliver opening speech and introduce the agenda for the symposium. The symposium will conclude with a closing ceremony, with videos reviewing the event and certificates awarded.

Welcome Party

On the first night of the symposium, a dinner will be held in the banquet hall of Cosmos Hotel Taipei to welcome medical students from all over the world. Ice-breaking games and further activities will be held to allow participants to get acquainted with each other for the upcoming days.

Farewell Party

Performances and drinks will be provided during the last night to send all medical students off happily.

City Tour

A city tour of Taiwan will be held to introduce historic sites to the students, from the famous Jiufeng neighbourhood, to allow students to understand the cultural significance of Taiwan.
